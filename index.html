Three independent public federal sources. Load as many as you want — results merge and de-duplicate. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is sent to a server.

CMS CLIA laboratories

Every CLIA-certified lab in the state: name, address, certificate type. The core list — includes high-complexity labs.

NPPES NPI-registered labs

National Provider Identifier registry. Best phone-number coverage of the three — this is where you get numbers to dial.

openFDA Device & IVD firms

FDA-registered device establishments — diagnostics manufacturers and biotech firms with regulated products.
